# Payment Methods

## Cash on delivery
Cash on delivery is available on all orders — pay in cash when your order arrives.

## Online payment
An online payment option is also available at checkout. If it isn't available for your order, cash on delivery is always an option.

## Payment security
No payment card details are stored on Aurevo Fashion's own servers.

## Order confirmation
Once an order is placed, a confirmation is sent to the email address provided at checkout, including the order number, items, and total.
