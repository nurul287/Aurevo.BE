# Returns & Exchanges

## Return window
Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are defective, damaged, or different from what was ordered. Products must be unused, unwashed, and returned in their original packaging with all tags attached. Please inspect your order at the time of delivery and raise any visible damage or incorrect items before the deliveryman leaves your premises.

## How to start a return
Contact us at aurevofashion88@gmail.com with your order number and photos of the item. Once approved, we'll arrange a pickup or provide a drop-off address.

## Refunds
Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method (or as store credit at your option) within 7–10 business days of us receiving the returned item.

## Non-returnable items
For hygiene reasons, items such as innerwear, socks, and pierced jewelry are not eligible for return or exchange. Sale, clearance, or customized items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged unless they arrive defective.

## Exchanges
Size or color exchanges are handled as a return plus a new order, subject to stock availability of the requested size/color.
