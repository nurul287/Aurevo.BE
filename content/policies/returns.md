# Returns & Exchanges

## Return window
Items can be returned or exchanged within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached.

## How to start a return
Contact us with your order number and the reason for the return. Once approved, we'll arrange a pickup or provide a drop-off address.

## Refunds
Approved returns are refunded to the original payment method (or as store credit for cash-on-delivery orders) within 5–7 business days of the returned item being received and inspected.

## Non-returnable items
Final-sale items, and any item marked as non-returnable on its product page, cannot be returned or exchanged.

## Exchanges
Size or color exchanges are handled as a return plus a new order, subject to stock availability of the requested size/color.
