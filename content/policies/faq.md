# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do you have a physical store?
Aurevo Fashion currently operates as an online-only store.

## Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?
Contact us as soon as possible with your order number. Once an order has shipped, it can no longer be changed or cancelled — a return can be requested instead once it arrives.

## Do I need an account to order?
No — guest checkout is available. Creating an account lets you save addresses and view your order history in one place.

## How do I track my order?
Log in and view the order in your account, or use the guest order lookup with your order number and email if you checked out as a guest.

## What if an item I want is out of stock?
Out-of-stock sizes/colors are shown as unavailable on the product page. Check back later, as restocks happen periodically.
